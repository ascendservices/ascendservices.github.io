What We Offer

Audit & Assurance

Critical role in maintaining investor and authority confidence. We offer IFRS Audit, Limited Reviews, Internal Audits, VAT Audits, RERA certifications, and Statutory accounts to ensure quality and compliance.

Taxation & Regulatory Requirements

We guide clients through the UAE's evolving tax landscape, assisting with **Corporate Taxation**, VAT registration/deregistration, Economic Substance Regulation (ESR), Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) requirements, and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Customized accounting solutions, including Bookkeeping, VAT accounting assistance, Management accounts, Payroll assistance, and implementation of new accounting software to maximize internal efficiency.

Advisory

Objective and independent advice to unlock your business's highest potential. Services include Management Consulting, Transaction Advisory & Restructuring, Financial Due Diligence, Business Valuation, and Diagnostic Studies.

Company Secretarial & Other Services

Specialist support for complex legal and corporate procedures, including Corporate Secretarial services, Advisory on HR/Employment matters, Company Formation/Liquidation, and guidance on Corporate Governance.
